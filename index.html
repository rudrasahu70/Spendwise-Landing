The Problem

Why people lose track
of their money

UPI made payments effortless — but nobody built the tool to make sense of all those transactions.

🕳️

UPI spending not tracked

You pay via UPI dozens of times a week, but there's no single view showing you where all that money actually went.

☕

Small expenses ignored

₹50 here, ₹120 there — they feel trivial, but they silently add up to thousands every month you never consciously chose to spend.

📉

No actionable insights

Bank SMS messages and statements are data dumps. You need patterns, category breakdowns, and smart alerts — not rows of merchant codes.

The Process

Four steps to total clarity

From scanning a QR to full financial insights — in seconds.

1
📷

Scan QR Code

Open SpendWise, point at any UPI QR before you pay. Takes under a second.

2
🏷️

Tag Category

One tap to tag — Food, Travel, Shopping. AI auto-suggests based on the merchant.

3
⚡

Pay Normally

Proceed with your usual UPI app. SpendWise logs it silently in the background.

4
📊

Get Insights

View weekly trends, category charts, and smart budget alerts that actually help.
